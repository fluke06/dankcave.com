Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what information Dankcave collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using our site you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly and information gathered automatically as you browse:

  • Contact and order details — name, shipping address, email, and phone number when you place an order.
  • Payment information — processed securely by our payment providers; we never store full card numbers.
  • Age verification — confirmation that you are 21 years or older, as required by law.
  • Usage data — pages visited, device and browser type, and similar analytics collected via cookies.

2. How we use your information

We use the information we collect to fulfill orders, provide customer support, improve our store, prevent fraud, and — only with your consent — send you marketing about drops and deals. You can opt out of marketing at any time.

3. Cookies

We use cookies to remember your age verification, keep items in your cart, and understand how the site is used. You can disable cookies in your browser, though some features may not work as intended.

4. Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with service providers who help us run the store — shipping carriers, payment processors, and analytics tools — under agreements that protect your data.

5. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information, or to opt out of certain data uses. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.

6. Age restriction

This site and its products are intended only for adults 21 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 21.

7. Contact us

Questions about this policy? Email support@dankcave.com or write to Dankcave, 3941 Holly Drive Suite J, Tracy, CA 95304.
